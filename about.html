Our Story

Innovation Born in the Heart of Kathu

In the heart of the bustling mining town of Kathu in the Northern Cape, K-Pre Projects & Events emerges as a beacon of innovation and determination within the Events Management Industry.

Founded in May 2019 by the visionary Enne-iles Pretorius, this dynamic company has quickly transcended its initial focus on children's parties to become a multifaceted enterprise. Today, it specializes in a broad spectrum of services including event management, décor, project management, business management, and supplies, catering to a diverse clientele.

From its inception, K-Pre Projects & Events has been fuelled by a relentless drive to exceed client expectations and achieve new heights of success. Despite its youth, the company has already made significant strides in reshaping the landscape of events management.

Navigating the tumultuous waters of the COVID-19 pandemic, K-Pre Projects & Events demonstrated resilience and adaptability, not only surviving but thriving amid global challenges. This period of adversity catalysed the expansion of their service offerings to encompass larger functions and corporate events, proving that obstacles can pave the way for growth and innovation.

Our Leader

Enne-iles Pretorius

Founder & Visionary

Enne-iles Pretorius is the visionary force and founding spirit behind K-Pre Projects and Events. With a solid educational foundation in Financial Accounting and Project Management, Enne-iles combines her technical expertise with an inherent creative flair and an unyielding drive for excellence.

This unique blend of skills allows her to oversee projects and events with a meticulous eye, ensuring that each one is executed with the utmost professionalism and efficiency. Under her leadership, K-Pre Projects and Events thrives on a culture of dedication, reliability, and loyalty.

Beyond her professional achievements, Enne-iles is a devoted mother to two energetic children. Her experiences in parenthood have honed her skills in multitasking, efficiency, and responsibility, further enriching her approach to business and client service.
