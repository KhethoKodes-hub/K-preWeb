FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for smaller events and 3-6 months for larger corporate events or weddings. However, we understand that sometimes events come up quickly, so we'll always do our best to accommodate last-minute requests.

Do you handle events outside of Kathu?

Yes, we regularly service events throughout the Northern Cape and can arrange services in other regions. Travel fees may apply for locations beyond a 50km radius from Kathu.

Can you work within my budget?

Absolutely! We pride ourselves on creating exceptional events that align with your budget. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your budget parameters and create a customized plan that delivers maximum value.

What makes K-Pre different from other event companies?

Our unique combination of project management expertise, creative flair, and personalized service sets us apart. As a local Kathu business, we have strong community connections and understand the specific needs of our region. Our founder's background in both financial management and creative events gives us a unique perspective on delivering successful events.

Do you provide services for both corporate and private events?

Yes, we specialize in both corporate and private events. From business conferences and team building to weddings and birthday parties, we have the expertise to make any event memorable and successful.
