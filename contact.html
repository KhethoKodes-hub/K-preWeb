FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to questions you might have about working with us

How soon can you respond to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly at 084 839 2852.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer complimentary initial consultations to discuss your event needs, whether in person, over the phone, or via video call.

What areas do you service?

While we're based in Kathu, we service events throughout the Northern Cape and can arrange services in other regions. Additional travel fees may apply for locations beyond a 50km radius.

Can I visit your showroom or warehouse?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors to our location at 4894 Sonneblom Avenue in Kathu. We recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure we can give you our full attention.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the service and how far in advance you cancel. We discuss all terms during the booking process to ensure complete transparency.
